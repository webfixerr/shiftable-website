Learn about
Shiftable

SHIFTABLE is a members-only Dental temping community and app that connects thriving dental practices to on-demand dental hygienists & dental assistants. Dental practices get access to reliable and vetted dental professionals for free and Dental temps get the flexibility of on-demand shifts that match their skills.

how do Temps get paid?

We don’t have a payment interface/ transaction fees involved yet. However, Temps get to raise an invoice post their shift inside the SHIFTABLE app and send it over to the dental practice to get paid.

how do dental practices hire temps?

SHIFTABLE allows dental practices to post job/shift requirements at zero upfront cost and hundreds and thousands of registered dental temps can apply based on their preferences. Dental practices can then select the suitable applicant, manage their shifts.
