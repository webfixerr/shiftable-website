How do temps get paid?

Shiftable offers a seamless and transparent temping experience for both dental practices and temps. Temps can raise invoices and send them directly to the dental practice via the Shiftable app after their shift, ensuring a fully documented and efficient process for both parties.

How do dental practices hire temps?

With Shiftable, dental practices can post job and shift requirements with zero upfront costs, and have access to thousands of registered dental temps who can apply based on their preferences. Dental practices can then easily select the best applicant, manage their shifts, and rate the temp based on their experience through the convenience of the Shiftable app.
